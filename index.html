I.T Summit

DAY 1: 5th I.T Summit and Pageant

This is 5th I.T Summit of College of Information Technology and Computer Studies (CITCS). The event held in April 19, 2020 at Ayala Malls South Park in Alabang Muntinlupa City. This is my first I.T summit and I learned a lot from two speakers which tackles about data mining and Adobe Photoshop. I will not regret to attend this seminar because it will taught us the proper techniques and by choosing our own programming language like python if we are wanted to be focus in analytics, php for web and java for desktop and game application.

DAY 1: Pageant ( BSIT vs ACT vs BSCS )

CITCS Department showcase the talent of candidates from BSIT, BSCS, and ACT representatives. It highlights the beauty,brain and dedication of each contestant. It also brings out that they're not just for academic but they can also stand out when it comes to other activities.

Opening

Day 2: Opening for Indoor and Outdoor Games

The "CITCS Days" with the theme "Bridge Gaps through IT Education: Fostering Industry Needs towards Sustainable Development" were officially open led by Sir Ricky Dimaapi, who gave an inspirational speech for the students. CITCS Pep Squad also take part in the opening ceremony performing their cheerdance to entertained the students. There are also some booth that sells affordable food and drinks.

DAY 2 & 3: Indoor Games and Contest

The indoor games or contest are composed of IT Quiz Bee, Chess, C++ and Java Programming, Word factory, web design and mobile games like Mobile legends with the participants coming from ACT, CS, and BSIT courses. This contest helping the CITCS students to be more competitive not only in academics but for their passion they wanted to be for their future.

Outdoor

DAY 2 & 3: Outdoor Games and Contest

The outdoor games is one of the main event for every students in CITCS. This event help the students to be stress-free in their academic for a mean while.
